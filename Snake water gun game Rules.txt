Water SNAKE Gun
# Rules:-
# Snake vs. Water: Snake drinks the water hence snake win the game.
# Water vs. Gun: The gun will drown in water, hence water win the game
# Gun vs. Snake: Gun will kill the snake and win the game.
# In situations where both players choose the same object, the result will be a draw.
